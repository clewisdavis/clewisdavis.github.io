Add Household Calendar Events

The household calendar shows events that pertain to all users in the household. All events on the household calendar appear on the Planner of each user in the household. In this step, review the household calendar.

If you are running the Scheduler for all users in the household, you can customize the household calendar by adding or removing personal events (e.g., a family vacation). Then, you can schedule lessons around these events. You may also be able to add or remove school calendar events.

When you've finished reviewing or making changes to your household calendar, you're ready to continue on to scheduling lessons.

Calendar Events

  • Add an event by clicking on a calendar date or using your keyboard to move to a date and hitting the Enter key.
  • To edit an existing event, select it from the calendar.
  • To include school events on your household calendar, select Add or Remove Calendars to choose which school calendars are added to your own.
  • Depending on whether your student's course schedule is set or customizable, you may not be able to block lessons using household events.
  • Adding personal events to the calendar will not change a course's end date, so be mindful of your student's workload.
