Scheduler

Welcome to the Scheduler! Use this tool to arrange lessons on your student's Planner or reschedule existing ones.

Before beginning...

  • Make sure you've marked off any lessons that are pending complete so that your student's progress doesn't get lost.

When you run the Scheduler, any overdue lessons will be rescheduled from the start date you select.
Note: If a teacher determines it is appropriate, he or she may skip lessons for a student.

Running the Scheduler

  1. 1Select Students

  2. 2Set Household Calendar

    If you are running the Scheduler for your entire household, you can customize your household calendar by adding personal events (e.g., family vacation). This will help schedule lessons around those events.

  3. 3Choose Start Date

    Select the first date when you want new or rescheduled lessons to appear on the Planner. Any incomplete or overdue lessons will be rescheduled starting on this date.

  4. 4Review or Rearrange Lessons

    Review the schedule we have created for you, or make edits to match your preference.

  5. 5Run the Scheduler

    This step will update your student's Planner with their new schedule.

Questions or concerns about finishing on time? Contact a teacher.
