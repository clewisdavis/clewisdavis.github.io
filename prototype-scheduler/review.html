Lesson Distribution

  • Editing lesson distribution is optional, but allows for greater scheduling flexibility.
  • If you choose to edit, refer to Lessons per Week Needed for Completion to make sure your student stays on track.
  • Your student's results may vary, but one lesson is meant to take about one hour to complete.
  • Lessons in fixed-schedule courses may not be redistributed.
