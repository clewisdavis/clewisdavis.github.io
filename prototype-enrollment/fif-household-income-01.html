Household Income

Your school may receive Title I funds for selected programs, benefits, or other services based on the information you provide in this section. If your family meets these federal guidelines, you will be required to provide a completed and signed copy of the 2014-2015 Family Income Form.

How many people live in the household?

A household includes all related family members and all unrelated persons, if any, such as foster children, wards, or employees, who share the housing unit.
