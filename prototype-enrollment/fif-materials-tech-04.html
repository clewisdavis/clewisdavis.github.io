Technology Agreements

Do you authorize Tech Support to access your personal computer to resolve technical issues?

In some programs, Connections Education provides our computers to students to use while enrolled. We also allow any registered user to access Connexus and other tools using their own personal computer as long as it meets the minimum technical specifications. Technical issues may arise which require our Tech Support staff to remotely access your personal computer to troubleshoot or attempt to resolve a problem. Since Connections Education does not own these computers, we have to make sure we are getting permission from the owner to access remotely when attempting to assist in resolving technical issues. In many cases, especially with our older students, we deal directly with the student, although it is very likely that the caretaker of that student is actually the one that purchased or "owns" the computer, so we want to be sure the caretaker has provided this permission.

NOTE: If you do not opt-in for this support we will be unable to assist your student directly if they have technical issues with your personal computer when the caretaker is unavailable--we will need the caretaker to contact us or be available over the phone for direct approval to access your personal computer before we can proceed with any assistance.
