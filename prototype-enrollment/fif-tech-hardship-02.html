Technology Acknowledgement

Do you need financial assistance for your internet bill?

The school's resources to provide Internet subsidies are limited. Therefore, eligibility is based upon whether or not each household meets the Federal low-income guidelines. If a student is enrolled for only part of the year, the payment will be prorated. Please consider carefully if you need this use of school resources. Your Internet service must meet the school's minimum technical requirements as listed in the School Handbook.
